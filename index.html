Xavier Math Games

Catch the Point

You are the blue point trying to catch the red. Every move, a random edge is removed. Try to corner the red point before an edge is removed and the points are separated. Switch to defense mode to try and evade red, and switch to two players to play with a friend.

Catch the Point

Triangle Push

The goal is to have a white triangle reach the top row of the board before a black triangle reaches the bottom row. Every move, a triangle can move one square up, down, left or right. Land on an opposing triangle to capture the piece. Try to contain the enemy pieces while finding a path to victory.

Triangle Push

Find the Basis

Vectors i and j form a unit square, and span 2D space. Drag and drop i' and j' to stretch, rotate, and flip the unit square so that the blue point falls on red. Press go to see where your transformation maps the blue point. Click solve to see how to get a (non unique) solution. Click help to learn more about how the game works.

Find the Basis

Graph the Match

The red points are you and your friends, and the blue points are another group of friends. Each person can talk to exactly one person at a time. The edge weights represent the quality of conversation between any two people. Highlight the set of edges that maximize the score for everyone involved. Press check to see if your matching is optimal.
